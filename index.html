Overview

Detailed geometry.
Consistent topology.

HiAnimal reconstructs high-fidelity 3D quadrupeds from a single in-the-wild image while producing clean, consistent meshes ready for downstream animation.

Our framework combines pixel-aligned implicit priors with the SMAL parametric model. An intermediate normal representation captures image-aligned geometric details, while a dense UV correspondence field aligns the parametric model to the reconstruction.
